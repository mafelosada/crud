Species

🐻 Bear
Carnivorous, plantigrade mammal.
🐯 Tiger
Carnivorous feline mammal native to Asia.
🦁 Lion
Carnivorous mammal, known as the "king of the jungle".
🐺 Wolf
Carnivorous mammal, ancestor of the domestic dog.
🦊 Fox
Small, cunning mammal of the canine family.
🐘 Elephant
The largest land mammal, with a trunk and tusks.
🦏 Rhinoceros
Large herbivorous mammal with a horn.
🦒 Giraffe
The tallest land mammal, with a long neck.
🦓 Zebra
African equine with black and white stripes.
🐼 Panda
Herbivorous mammal from China, famous for its black and white fur.
